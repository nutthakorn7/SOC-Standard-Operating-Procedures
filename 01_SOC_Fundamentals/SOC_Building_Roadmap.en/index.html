    SOC Building Roadmap ‚Äî From Zero to Operational¶

    Document ID: SOC-BUILD-001
    Version: 1.0
    Last Updated: 2026-02-15
    Audience: IT Managers, CISOs, Security Leaders starting a SOC from scratch

    Who Is This For?¶

    You have no SOC today. Maybe you have a small IT team that handles security "when something happens." This guide walks you through building a Security Operations Center from nothing ‚Äî step by step, phase by phase.

    Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1‚Äì3)¶

    1.1 Define Your SOC Mission¶

    Before buying anything, answer these questions:

    Question Why It Matters
    What are we protecting? Defines scope (servers, cloud, endpoints, OT?)
    What are our biggest risks? Prioritizes what to detect first
    What regulations apply? PDPA, PCI DSS, ISO 27001 affect requirements
    What's our budget? Determines build vs buy vs outsource
    Who will staff it? In-house vs MSSP vs hybrid

    1.2 Choose Your Operating Model¶

    Model Team Size Budget Best For
    üü¢ Hybrid MSSP 1‚Äì2 internal + MSSP ‡∏ø1.5‚Äì3M/yr Small org, <500 employees
    üü° Small In-house 3‚Äì5 analysts ‡∏ø5‚Äì10M/yr Mid-size, 500‚Äì2000 employees
    üî¥ Full In-house 8‚Äì15+ people ‡∏ø15‚Äì30M+/yr Enterprise, 2000+ employees

    Recommendation for beginners: Start with Hybrid MSSP ‚Äî outsource 24/7 monitoring, keep 1‚Äì2 people in-house for escalations and process building.
